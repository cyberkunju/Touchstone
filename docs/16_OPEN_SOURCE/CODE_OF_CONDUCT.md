# Code of Conduct

## 1. Our pledge

We want this project to be a respectful, safe, technically serious community for people building local-first document intelligence.

Contributors should be able to participate without harassment, personal attacks, discrimination, or hostile behavior.

---

## 2. Expected behavior

Expected:

- be respectful,
- assume good intent,
- critique ideas and code, not people,
- be patient with newcomers,
- keep discussions technical and constructive,
- respect privacy,
- do not pressure people to share sensitive documents,
- use synthetic/redacted examples.

---

## 3. Unacceptable behavior

Unacceptable:

- harassment,
- discriminatory language,
- threats,
- personal attacks,
- doxxing,
- sexualized language/images,
- posting real sensitive documents,
- pressuring users to reveal private data,
- sharing exploit details irresponsibly,
- intentionally adding privacy-breaking behavior.

---

## 4. Privacy-specific community rule

This project deals with sensitive documents. Do not ask contributors or users to upload real passports, IDs, bank statements, signatures, invoices, or private business documents.

Use synthetic or redacted samples.

---

## 5. Enforcement

Maintainers may:

- remove comments,
- close issues,
- reject PRs,
- temporarily or permanently ban participants,
- remove sensitive files posted by mistake.

---

## 6. Reporting

Report conduct issues through the security/contact method listed in `SECURITY.md` or project maintainer contact.

If sensitive documents were posted publicly, report immediately so maintainers can remove them.

---

## 7. Final note

Strong technical disagreement is welcome. Disrespect, privacy violations, and unsafe handling of sensitive documents are not.
